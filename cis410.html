Management of Information Systems

CIS 410

This course provided me with a broad overview of information systems management. It had a strong emphasis on IT planning, organizing, and controling. In this course, I was required to complete multiple case studies on large companies, and complete an organizational strategic report on an organization of my choice. The strategic report included a comprehensive hardware/software audit, and a gap analysis of how the organization can better utilize information technology to meet their future goals. I gained valuable experience in organizational analysis, information systems managment, strategic planning, and much more.

Key Topics Covered

  • Case analysis
  • Gap analysis
  • Strategic information use
  • Controlling the deployment of information technology
  • Information systems managment
